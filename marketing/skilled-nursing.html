24/7 Professional Care

Round-the-Clock Skilled Nursing and Long-Term Care

Skilled nursing is for those who require some form of 24-hour nursing care and support with the activities of daily living. For those who need support with personal care needs such as walking, eating, dressing, bathing, toileting, or transferring/standing up, skilled nursing communities can provide the necessary support.

Is This Right for You?

When Skilled Nursing May Be Right for Your Loved One

If your loved one is having trouble with multiple daily care activities or perhaps has a chronic illness, skilled nursing may be right for them. Our communities provide comprehensive care in a warm, supportive environment.

Signs That Skilled Nursing May Be Needed:

  • Difficulty with multiple activities of daily living
  • Managing chronic medical conditions
  • Requiring medication management
  • Need for 24-hour nursing supervision
  • Safety concerns at home

Financial Planning

How Do I Pay for Skilled Nursing?

Before you come to stay with us in an Optalis care community, we do our best to qualify you financially and go over the costs associated with skilled nursing to make sure you're able to afford it. Our experts will be able to answer your financial questions specific to your individual situation so you don't have to worry.

Payment Options May Include:

  • Medicare (for qualifying stays)
  • Private insurance
  • VA benefits (for Veterans)
  • Private pay
