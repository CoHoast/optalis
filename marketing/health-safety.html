About Optalis

Health & Safety

Our commitment to the health and safety of our residents, families, and team members.

Our Commitment

Keeping Our Communities Safe

At Optalis Healthcare, the health and safety of our residents, their families, and our dedicated team members is our top priority. We maintain rigorous infection prevention protocols and continuously update our practices based on the latest guidance from health authorities.

Our communities follow strict protocols for cleanliness, screening, and infection control to ensure a safe environment for everyone.

Protocols

Our Safety Measures

Enhanced Cleaning

Increased frequency of cleaning and disinfection of high-touch surfaces throughout all our communities.

Infection Prevention

Comprehensive infection prevention programs with regular staff training and monitoring.

Staff Screening

Daily health screening for all team members to ensure a safe environment for residents.

Visitor Guidelines

Thoughtful visitation policies that balance safety with the importance of family connections.

Vaccination Support

Coordination of vaccination clinics and support for residents and staff immunizations.

24/7 Monitoring

Round-the-clock nursing staff monitoring resident health and responding to any concerns.

Staying Healthy

Vaccination Information

Optalis Healthcare supports and encourages vaccinations as an important tool in protecting our residents and communities. We coordinate with local health departments to provide vaccination opportunities for residents and staff.

If you have questions about vaccinations or our health protocols, please contact your local Optalis community or reach out to our corporate office.
