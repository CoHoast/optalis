Compassionate, Person-Centered Care

Scattered throughout Ohio and Michigan, our healthcare and assisted living communities provide warm, welcoming environments for short-term guests and long-term residents with state-of-the-art care and therapy services. Each conveniently-located care community is like a neighborhood with its own culture and personality.

Our Care Services

We provide a full continuum of care designed to meet your unique needs at every stage of your healthcare journey.

Rehabilitation and Therapy

Our inpatient and outpatient rehabilitation and therapy programs offer custom support to ensure a successful recovery. We specialize in post-hospital rehabilitation and offer comprehensive services.

Learn More About Rehab

Skilled Nursing/Long-Term Care

For those who need support with personal care needs such as eating, dressing, bathing, toileting, or transferring/standing up, our long-term care communities provide comprehensive support. Hospice care is also available.

Learn More About Skilled Nursing

Assisted Living

A residential option for seniors who need a little assistance with daily activities - but it's not a nursing home. Cranberry Park communities offer beautiful apartments and personalized care.

Learn More About Assisted Living

Independent Living

For seniors who want to live a carefree lifestyle with the peace of mind of care that's available if it's ever needed. Meals and housekeeping are provided, and residents are free to add in services like private duty or home health.

Learn More About Independent Living

Specialized Care

Specialized care - including cardiac care, dialysis, respiratory care, and hospice - that ensures you're getting the exact care you need at select locations.

Learn More About Specialized Care

Memory Care

A secure, supportive environment for those with Alzheimer's or another type of dementia. Our specialized programs provide personalized care in a safe setting.

Learn More About Memory Care

Respite Care

Offers a break for caregivers and peace of mind that their loved ones are in a safe environment for short-term stays. Professional care while you rest.

Learn More About Respite Care

Veterans Care

For those who bravely served our country, Optalis is honored to contract with the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) to ensure timely, high-quality, and comprehensive rehabilitation and long-term care services tailored to individual needs.

Learn More About Veterans Care
