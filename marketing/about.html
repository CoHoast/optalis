Our Story

Grounded in Purpose. Guided by Principle.

For over 30 years, Optalis has been dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare and rehabilitation services across Ohio and Michigan. Our mission is simple: to provide compassionate, personalized care that helps our residents achieve their highest level of wellness and independence.

Our Heritage

Over 30 Years of Caring

Since 1992, Optalis has grown from a single facility to a network of over 30 care communities across Ohio and Michigan. What hasn't changed is our unwavering commitment to providing personalized, compassionate care.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that every person deserves to be treated with dignity and receive the highest quality care tailored to their individual needs. This philosophy continues to guide us today as we expand our services and reach more communities.

Our Growth Timeline

  • 1992: Founded with our first care community
  • 2000s: Expanded across Ohio and into Michigan
  • 2010s: Launched Cranberry Park assisted living brand
  • Today: 30+ communities serving thousands of families
