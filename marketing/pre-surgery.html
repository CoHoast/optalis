Pre-Surgery Reservations

Knowing what to expect and that your room and medical team will be ready for you allows you to feel more confident as a surgical patient.

Plan Your Recovery Before Your Surgery

If you are considering or have scheduled surgery and would like to plan for your recovery needs, we recommend making a pre-surgery reservation at one of our communities.

No Financial Obligation

When you make a pre-surgery room reservation, there is no financial obligation should your surgery be canceled or postponed.

Why Make a Pre-Surgery Reservation?

  • Peace of Mind: Know that your room and care team will be ready and waiting for you
  • Better Planning: Prepare for your recovery with confidence
  • No Surprises: Understand your care plan before you arrive
  • Family Assurance: Your loved ones will know where you'll be recovering

What's Included in Your Stay

When you choose Optalis for your post-surgery recovery, you'll have access to:

  • Physical, occupational, and speech therapy
  • 24-hour nursing care
  • Comfortable private or semi-private rooms
  • Chef-prepared meals with dietary accommodations
  • Activities and enrichment programs
  • Personal laundry and linen service
  • Transportation services

How to Make a Reservation

  1. Contact Us: Reach out to your preferred Optalis location or fill out our contact form
  2. Share Your Surgery Details: Let us know your surgery date and type of procedure
  3. Tour the Facility: We encourage you to visit and see where you'll be recovering
  4. Complete the Reservation: Our team will guide you through the simple process

Common Surgeries We Support Recovery For

Orthopedic

Hip replacement, knee replacement, spinal surgery

Cardiac

Heart valve, bypass, pacemaker

Neurological

Stroke recovery, brain surgery

General

Abdominal, cancer treatment recovery
