Specialized Memory Care

Safe, Supportive Care for Those With Alzheimer's or Dementia

Although most individuals with dementia experience memory loss, increased confusion, and difficulty communicating and carrying out everyday tasks, it's important to recognize that no two cases of Alzheimer's or dementia are exactly alike. They affect people in different ways, which is why we provide a personalized plan of care for each resident.

Our Heritage

Program Started in 1992

Our program offers a unique and effective approach to Alzheimer's and dementia care and was started in 1992 by Tricia Bingham, who has served as the Director of Programs and Services at the Alzheimer's Association Central Ohio Chapter.

With over 30 years of experience in memory care, we understand the complexities of cognitive disorders and have developed comprehensive programs that address the unique needs of each individual.

How Does Memory Care Work?

Memory care is specifically designed to provide safety and support to those with Alzheimer's or other types of dementia.

Structured Activities

Specialized programming offers residents a variety of structured activities throughout the day, which minimizes anxiety and agitation.

Secure Environment

An enclosed courtyard provides security while allowing safe outdoor enjoyment and connection with nature.

Social Engagement

Social engagement promotes emotional support and helps maintain cognitive function through meaningful connections.

Compassionate Staff

Our compassionate staff members understand the needs of residents experiencing cognitive disorders.
