Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our care services and the admission process.

How long will I stay for rehabilitation?

The average length of stay varies based on individual needs, medical condition, and recovery progress. Medicare typically covers up to 100 days of qualified rehabilitation services per benefit period.

What should I bring to rehabilitation?

Bring comfortable, loose-fitting clothing, proper footwear, any assistive devices you use, and a list of current medications. Our team will provide a complete admission checklist.

Can I visit and tour before admission?

Absolutely! We encourage tours and visits. Contact any of our communities to schedule a personalized tour and meet with our admissions team.

Do you accept my insurance?

We work with Medicare, most Medicare Advantage plans, private insurance carriers, and VA benefits. Our admissions team will verify your coverage and explain your benefits.

What therapies are available?

We offer physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech-language pathology, and specialized programs. Our partnership with Rivetus Rehabilitation ensures comprehensive, outcome-driven services.

How do I make a pre-surgery reservation?

Contact the community where you'd like to recover, and our team will reserve a room for you. There's no financial obligation if your surgery is canceled or postponed.

Need More Information?

Our experienced team is here to answer your questions and help you understand all your options. Contact us for personalized guidance.
