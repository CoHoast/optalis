Specialized Services

Advanced Specialized Care

Expert care for complex medical needs, including cardiac rehabilitation, dialysis support, respiratory therapy, and compassionate hospice services.

Schedule a Consultation Find a Location

Cardiac Care

Comprehensive cardiac rehabilitation and monitoring for patients recovering from heart surgery, heart attacks, or managing chronic heart conditions.

  • Post-surgical cardiac rehabilitation
  • Heart failure management
  • Continuous cardiac monitoring
  • Cardiovascular exercise programs
  • Medication management

Dialysis Support

Coordinated care for residents requiring dialysis treatment, with transportation arrangements and post-treatment monitoring.

  • Dialysis scheduling coordination
  • Transportation assistance
  • Pre and post-treatment monitoring
  • Dietary management
  • Fluid balance monitoring

Respiratory Care

Specialized respiratory therapy and support for patients with COPD, post-ventilator care, and other pulmonary conditions.

  • Ventilator weaning programs
  • Oxygen therapy management
  • Breathing treatments
  • Tracheostomy care
  • Pulmonary rehabilitation

Hospice Partnership

Compassionate end-of-life care in partnership with leading hospice providers, ensuring comfort and dignity for residents and families.

  • Pain and symptom management
  • Emotional and spiritual support
  • Family counseling services
  • 24/7 on-call hospice support
  • Bereavement services
