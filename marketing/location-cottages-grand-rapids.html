About The Cottages

Create the Life You Want

At The Cottages at Optalis of Grand Rapids, every senior has the opportunity to create the life they want. With beautiful, spacious one- and two-bedroom floor plans and month-to-month leases, you'll be thrilled to call The Cottages your home.

Enjoy the freedom of independent living with the peace of mind that comes from being on a full continuum of care campus. Access activities, dining, and health services whenever you need them.
