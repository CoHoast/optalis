About Independent Living

Freedom Without the Hassles

Our Cranberry Park communities offer active adults the perfect blend of independence and convenience. Say goodbye to yard work, home repairs, and the isolation that can come with living alone.

Instead, enjoy beautifully appointed apartments, resort-style amenities, engaging social activities, and the peace of mind that comes with having support nearby if you ever need it.
