Understanding Veterans Affairs Aid and Attendance Benefits

Wartime Veterans and their surviving spouses may be eligible for a monthly benefit to help pay for the cost of assisted living care in our Cranberry Park locations.

Who Is Eligible?

  • Wartime Veterans
  • Surviving spouses of eligible Veterans
  • Veterans or surviving spouses who require assistance with activities of daily living
  • Veterans or surviving spouses who first meet the eligibility requirements for a standard VA pension

Monthly Benefits Available

The Veterans Affairs Aid and Attendance benefits provide monthly payments in addition to a monthly VA pension to help cover assisted living costs.
