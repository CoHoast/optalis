About This Community

Where Care Meets Community

Cranberry Park at Milford provides assisted living and memory care in the charming village of Milford. Our boutique community offers personalized attention in a setting that feels like home.

With beautiful surroundings and a dedicated care team, residents enjoy independence while receiving the support they need to thrive.
